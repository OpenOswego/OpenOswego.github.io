    Welcome to Open Oswego.

    The idea behind this project it to create a platform for SUNY Oswego CS graduates to communicate ideas, show case group and independent projects, and encourage open source development of all kinds!

    What does open source mean?

    Open source software and hardware are designed to be freely used and modified by anyone.
    Take a look at: http://opensource.org/

    Can I help?

    YES! Right now the Open Oswego project is currently setting up its core group of developers with several initial goals in mind, including the scope of the project. But if you have ideas or want to talk about becoming a developer feel free to contact Mark Williams (@mwilli22).

    Authors and Contributors

    Currently Mark Williams (@mwilli22) is the primary contact for this project. As more developers and contributors join you will see them listed here.
